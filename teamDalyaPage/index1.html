Dalya Altorok

Aka: "Frog"

Concept Artist

Meet Dalya the Frog, the slightly unhinged concept artist at Glitch 'n' Ditch Studios. Armed with a sketchpad in one webbed hand and a dubious amount of caffeine in the other, she crafts game worlds as vivid as her own fever dreams. Between brainstorming sessions and existential crises, she swears her croaks are getting louder — but that’s probably just the lack of sleep... right? Someone please check on her.
